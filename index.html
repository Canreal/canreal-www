Canreal - The Hytale MMORPG

Canreal is THE Hytale MMORPG-Server currently in development.
Join us, so you won't miss any updates! Because Canreal is Open Source
you can also contribute to the project to be a part of it!

WHAT TO DISCOVER ON CANREAL?

Awesome adventures

There will be many awesome adventures on Canreal! Fight monsters, do quests, grind for special items, discover the world with your friends, and much more!

"But don't fall in dark holes..."
A huge map

You thought you played games with a huge map and much to dicsover? Then you haven't played on Canreal, yet. The world will be huge. There are many things you can discover! - Yes, you can find secrets, too!

"Be sure to take your horse with you!"
Unique creatures

Hytale gives us a huge amount of creatures. On Canreal these will be modified by us, so that every creature is unique on Canreal! There will be weak creatures, nice creatures, strong creatures, evil creatures, dumb creatures and much more! For some you will need to collect or craft unique items, armor, weapons and more to be stronger, for others you only need a weak sword you took from a poor stranger.

"Ssshh...don't tell anyone where to find the good stuff..."
